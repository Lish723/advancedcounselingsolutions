Welcome Process

On arrival, our friendly team will welcome you and guide you through a brief 3–5 minute intake form in our comfortable lobby. Please have your insurance and basic details ready.

Guardian Requirements

If you are under the age of 18 you will need to bring a parent or guardian to your appointment. Guardians will need to bring documentation that proves they are the legal guardian.

Nutrition Support

In partnership with LS Nutrition, we provide access to registered dietitian services that support emotional health and physical well-being. A consistent, balanced diet may help reduce mood swings and ease symptoms of anxiety and depression.

Wellness Complete

Our team promotes sustainable self-care practices that nourish the mind and body. We guide clients in building clarity, emotional grounding, and habits that support long-term resilience.

Accountability for Growth

Personal growth is a process. We provide structure, encouragement, and goal-setting support to help individuals stay focused and intentional as they evolve.

Our Sports Performance Enhancement offering supports young athletes in building mental resilience, sharpening focus, and developing positive self-talk. These skills are key to thriving under pressure—on the field, in training, and beyond.

Through guided enrichment groups, we address internal barriers that affect performance and provide strategies to foster a growth mindset, emotional regulation, and consistent motivation.
